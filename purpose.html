Our Purpose

Our purpose is to present the requirement of the welfare system and shelters for the animals. The project thus takes information of the animals and help them provide shelter or if injured then provide the proper medication to them. It does almost every work which is related to animals like- providing shelter, provide food, provide medication and proper care of them will be taken. "Little paws welfare system " is being made to help the animals that can't get enough food or the dog that had been injured and to help the person who wants to help these dogs but can't help because of taking long term care or due to expenses.
